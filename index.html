3. Closebot

Closebot handles conversational AI across Facebook Messenger, SMS, and Email channels. It is the primary lead engagement bot for most LeadStacker clients.

3.1 Core Setup Steps

  1. Connect the client's Facebook Page or GHL sub-account
  2. Configure the bot persona (name, tone, AI identity disclosure)
  3. Set accurate business hours matching client availability
  4. Define the offer details — verify with client before programming
  5. Set up abuse detection and opt-out triggers
  6. Save AND publish — confirm live status after publish

3.2 Closebot QA Checklist

  • Bot persona and name configured per client brief
  • Business hours set correctly (Monday through Sunday format)
  • Opt-out / STOP trigger confirmed working
  • Abuse detection enabled
  • Offer details verified with client
  • Bot saved and published (not draft)
  • Settings re-opened post-publish to confirm no revert
  • Test conversation completed end-to-end
⚠️ Common Issue Many support tickets come from bots left in draft. Always confirm "Published" status after saving.

4. CommentGuard

CommentGuard manages and engages with Facebook ad comments using AI, protecting brand reputation and driving leads from comment threads.

4.1 What It Does

  • Auto-responds to comments on Facebook ads and posts
  • Hides or flags spam and negative comments for review
  • DMs engaged commenters to continue the conversation
  • Captures leads from organic comment activity

4.2 Setup Checklist

  • Facebook Page connected with correct permissions
  • Ad account linked and ad sets selected
  • Comment reply tone and persona configured
  • Spam/negative sentiment filters active
  • DM follow-up sequence configured
  • Test comment posted and response verified
  • Client notified of what gets auto-hidden vs. flagged

4.3 Escalation Rules

TriggerAction
Negative sentiment detectedFlag for human review, do not auto-reply
Complaint or refund requestHide comment, alert account manager
Competitor mentionFlag only — no automated response
Spam / bot commentAuto-hide immediately

5. Synth — AI SMS Chatbot

🚀 LeadStacker Proprietary Technology Custom-built AI SMS system combining n8n automation, Claude AI, and GoHighLevel.

5.1 What Synth Does

  • Texts new leads immediately from ads or forms
  • Real conversations — handles objections, remembers context
  • Drives leads toward conversion (forms, bookings, calls)
  • Auto-tags and tracks everything in GHL
  • Multi-tenant — one system serves all clients

5.2 Architecture

Facebook Ad / Website Form
    ↓
GHL Sub-Account (client's account)
    ↓  webhook fires, includes locationId
n8n — master workflow pair
    ↓  looks up client config by locationId
Claude API — Jamie persona, built dynamically
    ↓  returns: SMS reply + JSON actions
n8n executes actions (tags, pipeline updates)
    ↓
GHL sends SMS back to lead
    ↓
Lead replies → loops back through chain

5.3 Lead Scoring & Sentiment

Score / SignalTag AppliedAction
Score ≥ 8synth_high_intentPrioritize for human follow-up
Score 4–7synth_medium_intentContinue nurture sequence
Score 1–3synth_low_intentDeprioritize, minimal follow-up
Negative sentimentsynth_escalateAlert human team immediately

5.4 Booking Confirmation Loop

  • Wait 3 hours after booking link is sent
  • Auto-check if booking was completed in GHL
  • Send one friendly follow-up if not completed
  • Tag accordingly and stop automated outreach

5.5 Synth QA Checklist

  • Config added to BOTH n8n workflows
  • locationId correct (exact match from GHL)
  • Business hours match client availability
  • Conversion link tested and working
  • GHL Automation published
  • GHL Webhook configured correctly
  • Test SMS received within 2 minutes
  • Tags applied correctly in GHL
  • STOP keyword triggers opt-out

6. GHL Integration

All bots connect into GoHighLevel as the central CRM. Correct GHL setup is required before any bot goes live.

6.1 Required GHL Setup

ItemRequirement
Sub-account createdOne per client — never share sub-accounts
Webhook URLConfigured in GHL triggers for inbound leads
Pipeline stagesMatch client's sales process before bot goes live
Custom fieldsMap bot data fields to GHL contact fields
TagsSynth tags pre-created: high/medium/low intent, escalate
AutomationPublished — not draft

6.2 Webhook Test

  1. Submit a test lead through the client's form or ad
  2. Confirm webhook fires in n8n execution log
  3. Confirm contact created in GHL within 60 seconds
  4. Confirm bot response sent (SMS/Messenger)
  5. Confirm correct tags applied to GHL contact

7. Client Onboarding

Follow this checklist for every new client before any bot is activated.

7.1 Pre-Build Requirements

  • Client brief received and reviewed
  • Offer details confirmed in writing with client
  • Facebook Page access granted (if applicable)
  • GHL sub-account created and configured
  • Business hours confirmed in writing
  • AI identity preference confirmed (disclose vs. not)
  • Opt-out language approved by client

7.2 Go-Live Checklist

  • All QA checklists completed for active bots
  • End-to-end test conversation passed
  • Client walkthrough completed
  • Client signed off on bot behavior
  • Change log entry created
  • Account manager assigned and briefed
